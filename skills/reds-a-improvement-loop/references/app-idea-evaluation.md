# App Idea Evaluation

Use this when screening app ideas across the App Store, Google Play, and social platforms.

## Goal

Choose ideas that can be shipped fast, monetized clearly, and validated with observable demand.

## Scorecard

Score each category 1-5 and summarize in one short table or bullet list.

1. **Pain Intensity**
   - Is there a real recurring problem?
   - Are people already complaining, hacking around it, or paying to solve it?

2. **Urgency**
   - Do users want this now, or is it vague aspiration?
   - Would they search for it proactively?

3. **Monetization Clarity**
   - Ads, subscription, one-time purchase, upsell, lead gen?
   - Is there a believable path to revenue without fantasy CAC assumptions?

4. **Distribution Fit**
   - Can App Store / Play search, social content, or communities realistically drive discovery?
   - Is the topic memetic, searchable, or shareable?

5. **Build Speed**
   - Can a credible MVP ship in days or a few weeks?
   - Are APIs, models, or tooling available?

6. **Retention Potential**
   - Is there repeat use, habit, workflow stickiness, or recurring need?

7. **Competition Pressure**
   - Are incumbents weak, bloated, expensive, or badly designed?
   - Is the market crowded but sloppy, or impossible to crack?

8. **Defensibility / Wedge**
   - What is the angle: audience, feature, speed, taste, bundle, workflow?

## Fast Reject Rules

Reject or downgrade ideas if most of these are true:

- problem is vague or low-stakes
- monetization is hand-wavy
- requires huge network effects on day one
- depends on prohibited scraping or risky platform behavior
- incumbent products are already excellent and cheap
- validation evidence is mostly imagined, not observed

## Evidence Sources

Look for:

- App Store / Play reviews
- Reddit posts and comments
- X complaints, wishlists, recurring jokes, workflow screenshots
- Instagram/TikTok creator pain points
- SEO keywords and forum threads
- competitor pricing, churn complaints, and feature gaps

## Output Format

```markdown
APP IDEA SCREEN
- Idea:
- User:
- Problem:
- Evidence:
- Monetization:
- MVP:
- Risks:
- Score (8 categories):
- Verdict: pursue | watch | reject
```

## Bias

Prefer ideas with:

- clear pain
- ugly competitors
- simple MVP path
- fast validation
- obvious money
