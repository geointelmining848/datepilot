## 1. Profile Doctor sample

### Sample bio
Product designer. Better in person than over text, but still making an effort here. I like long walks, good coffee, strong opinions about terrible movies, and people who can actually hold a conversation.

### Sample prompts
**A shower thought I recently had:** half of adulthood is just becoming weirdly specific about coffee, lighting, and chairs.

**My ideal Sunday:** coffee, a bookstore, something outside, and dinner somewhere that doesn’t need to be posted.

**We’ll get along if:** you’re curious, a little funny, and don’t need three business days to suggest a plan.
