## Core positioning
DatePilot is a practical dating coach for people using dating apps like Tinder, Hinge, and Bumble. It helps users improve profiles, write better opening lines, coach replies, and ask for dates more naturally.

It is a coaching utility, not a dating app and not an automation bot.

## App Store description draft
DatePilot helps you get better matches and better conversations on dating apps.

Use DatePilot to:
- rewrite your profile with stronger hooks
- generate better opening messages
- coach your next reply in a live conversation
- decide when and how to ask for a date

DatePilot is designed for people who want practical help without cheesy pickup-line spam or awkward AI sludge.

FEATURES
- Profile Doctor: tighten your bio and prompt answers
- Opener Generator: create stronger first messages
- Reply Coach: get better next-message options
- Ask-Out Helper: know when to move from chat to real plans
- Saved style preferences: keep outputs closer to your tone

WHY DATEPILOT
- practical, not cringe
- focused on real conversation flow
- built for clarity, not automation gimmicks
- helps you move from match to actual date

IMPORTANT
DatePilot does not send messages for you and does not connect directly to third-party dating apps. It provides coaching and writing assistance only.

## Google Play full description draft
DatePilot is a practical dating coach for people who want better matches, better replies, and better odds of turning chats into real dates.

If your dating profile feels weak, your first messages feel generic, or your conversations keep stalling out, DatePilot helps you improve the parts you actually control.

With DatePilot you can:
- rewrite your profile and prompts
- generate stronger opening messages
- coach your next reply in a conversation
- get help asking someone out without making it awkward
- save your preferred tone and style for better future suggestions

DatePilot is built to feel useful, calm, and practical — not like a spammy pickup-line machine.

FEATURES
- Profile Doctor
Analyze your bio and prompts, then get a stronger rewrite and alternate versions.

- Opener Generator
Turn profile details into more natural, more specific first messages.

- Reply Coach
Paste a conversation snippet and get stronger next-reply options.

- Ask-Out Helper
Get a timing read and simple ask-out suggestions that feel natural.

- Saved Preferences
Keep your tone and goals consistent across the app.

WHAT DATEPILOT IS NOT
- not a dating app
- not a swipe bot
- not an auto-messaging tool
- not a third-party app integration layer

DatePilot gives you coaching and writing support only. You stay in control.
