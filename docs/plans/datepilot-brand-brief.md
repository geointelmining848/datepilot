# DatePilot Brand Brief

## Core brand idea
DatePilot should feel like a sharp, modern utility for dating-app performance — not a cheesy pickup-line toy and not a generic AI chatbot.

## Desired feeling
- practical
- confident
- premium
- clear
- calm
- slightly sharp

## Avoid
- neon-trash dating aesthetics
- pickup-artist energy
- manipulative tone
- oversexualized branding
- generic AI swirl logos
- cringe terms like "rizz machine"

## Visual direction
- dark, premium base
- high contrast accents
- purple / pink / cyan accents used carefully
- rounded but not childish
- clean cards and strong typography
- subtle glow, not loud gradients everywhere

## Icon direction
Best direction for now:
- clean DP monogram or
- abstract navigation / pointer / beacon motif
- avoid hearts as the primary symbol unless extremely restrained

## Splash direction
- dark background
- centered minimal mark
- quiet confidence, not loud animation energy

## Store screenshots tone
- concise headlines
- one message per screen
- outcome-oriented
- practical and trust-building

## Copy style
- no begging for attention
- no fake swagger
- no guaranteed results claims
- focus on stronger judgment, stronger writing, better momentum

## Product voice
"Date better with less guesswork."

"Sharper profiles. Better replies. Cleaner momentum."

"Practical dating coaching, not automation gimmicks."
